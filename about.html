About Elvis


Practicing massage since 2003 in various environments, Elvis Gardin, LMT, CPMT Licensed Massage Therapist, Certified Prenatal Massage Therapist is an Alumni of the renowned college accredited, Ivy league of schools for massage and wellness, Swedish Institute College of Applied Health Sciences located in New York, NY.

Elvis has a natural, confident knowledge of the human body coupled with wisdom through many years of consistent experience working with all types of people, pathologies and lifestyles using various modalities he has studied and practiced, tailored to each individual's needs.

Experience derived from a very broad clientele of massage connoisseurs ranging from contestants on the American Idols Tour 2006, many Broadway actors/dancers from Aida, Lion King, Hairspray, Wicked, and Fame. More than a few professional NFL players, prime time television sets, and private 1:1's with celebrity clients. This gained him a title of massage therapist to the stars. Elvis works with triathletes, and was sports massage therapist for Columbia University's Athletic Department for one year. He gives back in many ways to the community. Elvis started Hurricane Sandy Massage Relief Project compliments of Massage Wellness Haven in Monmouth and Ocean counties following the natural disaster. Displaced victims enjoyed the hospitality provided by a local church and raved about the free chair massages! First responders such as the National Guard were also extremely grateful in welcoming Elvis to set up shop to bring comfort and aide to men in uniform working around the clock.

Elvis holds over10 years of consistent, top grade massage experience to Corporate America, including 6 years dedication to Google Inc. NYC.
